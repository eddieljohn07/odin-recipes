Sisig Recipe

A spicy and meaty food that can be enjoyed any time of the day.

The best way to consume sisig is with a cup of warm white rice on the side, as it may also be served as a main dish. It can also be a side dish to combine while enjoying alcoholic beverages.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. pig ears
  • 1 1/2 lb pork belly
  • 1 piece onion minced
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 knob ginger minced (optional)
  • 3 tablespoons chili flakes
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 piece lemon or 3 to 5 pieces calamansi
  • ½ cup butter or margarine
  • ¼ lb chicken liver
  • 6 cups water
  • 3 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 piece egg

Steps

  1. Add salt and pepper to the boiling water after pouring it into the pan.
  2. Drain the excess water after removing the cooked items from the pot.
  3. Grill the pork ears and belly until satisfied.
  4. Chop it into small cubes.
  5. Melt the butter or margarine in a large pan. Include the onions. Cook onions until they are tender.
  6. Cook the ginger at the same pan for 2-3 minutes
  7. Add chicken liver then mash it while cooking.
  8. Add the cubed pork for 15-25 minutes.
  9. Add the condiments while mixing such as garlic powder, and soy sauce.
  10. Add salt and pepper according to your liking.
  11. Mix-in mayonnaise in the pan.
  12. Over to a serving dish after transfer. Add a raw egg and sliced green onions on top.
  13. Add calamansi. (optional)
