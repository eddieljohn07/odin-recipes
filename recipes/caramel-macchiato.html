Caramel Macchiato

A yummy drink that can be cold or hot!

Freshly steamed milk with vanilla-flavoured syrup, marked with espresso and finished with caramel sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 Espresso Roast
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp Vanilla Syrup
  • Caramel Sauce

Steps

  1. Pour 1 shot of espresso roast in a mug.
  2. Froth the milk on a medium setting.
  3. Pour Vanilla syrup in the bottom of the mug.
  4. Put frothed milk in the mug.
  5. Pour espresso shot through the frothed milk.
  6. Drizzle Caramel sauce on top
  7. Put ice on your drink. (optional)
